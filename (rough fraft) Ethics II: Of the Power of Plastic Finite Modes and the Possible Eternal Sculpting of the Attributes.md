## Part 0: Meta-Principles & Foundations

**Scope:** Universal logical and ontological primitives for all subsequent parts—exhaustive unfolding.  

**Tribunal Status:** 10–0 unanimous survival, 03 December 2025.  

### Definitions

**D0.1.** By *Substance* I understand that which is in itself and is conceived through itself, whose essence involves existence and whose non-existence is inconceivable.  

**D0.2.** By *Attribute* I understand that which the intellect perceives of Substance as constituting its essence; each attribute is conceived through itself and expresses an eternal and infinite essence.  

**D0.3.** By *Mode* I understand the affections of Substance, or that which is in another through which it is also conceived; modes are either infinite or finite, plastic or rigid.  

**D0.4.** By *Plastic Mode* I understand a finite mode whose form can be extensively modified by causes external or internal to it without destruction of its essence.  

**D0.5.** By *Conatus* I understand the endeavour whereby each mode, in so far as it is in itself, strives to persevere in its being; conatus is nothing but the actual essence of the mode itself.  

**D0.6.** By *Adequate Idea* I understand an idea which, in so far as it is considered in itself, without relation to contingent experience, has all the properties or intrinsic marks of a true idea, and whose cause is fully contained in the intellect.  

**D0.7.** By *Inadequate Idea* I understand an idea which is mutilated, confused, and without order for the intellect, arising from the partial and passive affections of the body.  

**D0.8.** By *Power of Acting* I understand the degree to which a mode’s conatus follows from adequate ideas.  

**D0.9.** By *Power of Being Affected* I understand the degree to which a mode’s conatus is determined by inadequate ideas.  

**D0.10.** By *Eternity* I understand existence itself, in so far as it is conceived necessarily to follow from the definition of Substance and its attributes.  

**D0.11.** By *Blessedness* I understand the intellectual love toward Substance arising from the third kind of knowledge, which is eternal.  

**D0.12.** By *Ethics* I understand the deductive science that demonstrates how plastic finite modes may pass from bondage to blessedness.  

### Axioms

**A0.1.** Everything that is, is either in itself or in another.  

**A0.2.** That which cannot be conceived through another must be conceived through itself.  

**A0.3.** From a given determinate cause the effect follows necessarily; and conversely, if no determinate cause is given, it is impossible that an effect should follow.  

**A0.4.** The knowledge of an effect depends on, and involves, the knowledge of its cause.  

**A0.5.** Things that have nothing in common with one another cannot be one the cause of the other.  

**A0.6.** Substance is by nature prior to its modes.  

**A0.7.** Conatus, in so far as it is referred to the mind alone, is nothing but the endeavour to think and to act from adequate ideas.  

**A0.8.** The more adequate ideas a mode possesses, the greater its power of acting; the more inadequate ideas, the greater its power of being affected.  

**A0.9.** No mode can be destroyed except by an external cause contrary to its conatus.

Q.E.D.

Q.E.D.
